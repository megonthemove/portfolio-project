About.

front-end developer, based in Colorado

Hi there, I'm Megan.

I've been in restaurant hospitality for 15 years now and have recently started to learn Front End Development at SheCodes in my spare time. I have loved every (challenging) minute of it and I am constantly learning new things and expanding my skill set. Coding allows me to bring my creativity to life in ways I never thought I would be able to. Before learning to code, Photography was my favorite outlet for creativity. It is the most satisfying thing to create something from scratch.

When I can walk away from learning how to code, I like to spend my time outdoors. Some of my hobbies include Mountain sports, jumping in alpine lakes, and traveling to beautiful places. I love early morning workouts and starting my day in the sun, the colors of autumn, healthy food, yoga and cuddles from my pup.

If you are interested in working together, reach out. I am looking forward to it!
